about intake

The web delivers a constant influx of uncontrolled information. There is a direct connection between exposure to negative media and content and increase of anxiety in consumers. Compassion fatigue is a form of trauma characterized by a lack of ability to sympathize with others due to this overabundance of negative content. intakestrives to help users remain aware of the content they are taking in and the effects this could have on their mental wellbeing by sending gentle reminders and providing analytics about your web activity.
